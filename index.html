Distafi

You at least need to know how long you hit the ball.

  1. Click the club you're hitting
  2. When the accuracy is acceptable (< 5?), click 'Confirm hit shot location'
  3. Walk to your ball and click 'Calculate distance'
  4. Check that the accuracy is acceptable and click 'Confirm shot end location'
  5. Repeat!
